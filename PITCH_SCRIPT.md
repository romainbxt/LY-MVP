## SLIDE 2 — The Story Hook (30s)
*[Slide: Francesca quote, dark background]*

> "Monday morning. 7:15. Francesca opens her coffee shop in Kreuzberg.
>
> Marco should be here by now. Double espresso, no sugar. Every Monday for three years. But Marco didn't come last Monday. Or the one before.
>
> Francesca remembers his birthday. His dog's name. The day he switched from cappuccino to espresso.
>
> But she had no way of knowing he was leaving. No alert. No signal. No second chance."

## SLIDE 3 — The Problem (30s)
*[Slide: 4 key statistics]*

> "Now multiply Francesca by 10 million. That's how many independent food and beverage businesses across Europe face this exact problem — every single week.
>
> 78% of restaurant guests never come back after their first visit. Each restaurant loses roughly 375,000 euros a year to silent churn. And acquiring a new customer costs 5 to 7 times more than keeping an existing one.
>
> These businesses are bleeding revenue — and they don't even know it."

## SLIDE 6 — How It Works (25s)
*[Slide: 4 steps]*

> "Here's how simple it is.
>
> Step 1: You print a QR code and put it at your counter. Takes 30 seconds.
>
> Step 2: Customers scan it. They enter their first name and phone number. They get their own personal QR code — no app download needed.
>
> Step 3: Every time they visit, your employee scans their QR. That's it. Visits tracked automatically.
>
> Step 4: LY watches the patterns. When Marco hasn't been back in 12 days — and he usually comes every 3 — you get an alert. With a suggested action. One WhatsApp message later, Marco is back."

## Tips for Delivery

1. **The opening story** is everything. Deliver it slowly. Make eye contact. Don't rush.
2. **On "But she had no way of knowing he was leaving"** — pause. Let it land.
3. **When showing the live product** — have lyloyal.com open on a phone to show during Q&A.
4. **The closing line** should be delivered slowly, with conviction. Pause between "the heart" and "the eyes."
5. **Practice 3 times** before presenting. Time yourself. Cut anything that feels forced.

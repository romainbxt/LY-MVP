Loyalty tiers explained

Tiers are calculated automatically based on visit count and frequency.

  • Bronze New customers, score < 20
  • Silver Regular visitors, score 20-49
  • Gold Loyal customers, score 50-99
  • Platinum VIP customers, score 100+

Score = total visits × (1 + visits per week). Customers who visit more often level up faster.
